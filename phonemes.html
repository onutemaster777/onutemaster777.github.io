Phonemes for FakeYou

If you try "RIT", it immediately cuts off. You must put "ar i tee" instead if "RIT".

(Update from November 1st, 2022: typo fixes)

Here's a list of phonemes:

  • q - kju
  • w - double you
  • e - ee
  • r - ar
  • t - tee
  • y - why
  • u - you
  • i - i
  • o - oh
  • p - pee
  • a - ay
  • s - es
  • d - dee
  • f - ef
  • g - gee
  • h - eich

  • k - kay
  • l - el
  • z - zee
  • x - eks
  • c - cee
  • v - vee
  • b - bee
  • n - en
  • m - em

    • Special character phonemes:

  • @ - at
  • # - hash (alt: number) (sometimes "pound")
  • $ - dollar
  • % - percent
  • & - and
  • * - star
  • + - plus
  • - - minus
  • / - slash
  • \ - backslash
  • : - colon
  • . - dot
